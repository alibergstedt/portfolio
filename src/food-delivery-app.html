Food Delivery Mobile App

Figma Animations

In the vastly changing landscape of UX, motion has become an integral part of any application. Motion and micro interactions can often be the difference between a user staying or leaving.

Motion helps tell a story and provides important feedback to a user. Without it, they may get frustrated and just abandon. I created this mock food delivery application through Udemy and Figma to show the power of animations, micro-interaction, and prototyping.
